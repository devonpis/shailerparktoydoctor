## Summary (quick reference)

**Shailer Park Toy Doctor** is a **home-based** toy and small-appliance repair service in **Shailer Park, QLD**. There is **no walk-in** service — customers **book / enquire first** (email or phone; **SMS preferred** on contact page).

**What we do**

- 🛠️ Giving old toys new life  
- 🧸 Plush cleaning & sewing repairs  
- 🤖 Electronic & mechanical fixes  
- 🎨 Anime figure & scale model paint jobs  

**Purpose / mission** (from site copy, paraphrased)

Bring cherished toys and small electronics back to life at an affordable price — for kids’ toys and “big kids’” gadgets — so families keep what they love, send less plastic and e-waste to landfill, and save money versus buying new. Tagline on home page: *“Bring back to life your toy, your memory, and our planet, at an affordable price.”*
