About us / our mission

Do you or your kids have a beloved toy or small electronic that is no longer working properly? Before it goes to landfill — at Toy Doctor we bring cherished toys back to life at a fraction of the cost of buying new, so families keep what they love and send less plastic and e-waste to landfill.

Do you know … Almost 90% of faulty electronics can be repaired and saved from becoming e-waste. The fix is often simple — poor contacts or a damaged component — but too costly for many shops. We aim to make repair affordable so people try fixing before replacing.

We cannot repair mobile phones, computers, tablets or laptops — please use a specialist for those.
