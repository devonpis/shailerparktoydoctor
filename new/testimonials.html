Can the website fetch reviews automatically?

Not easily on this static site. Google does not offer a free widget that lists all review text on your own page. The Places API can return a few reviews but needs a Google Cloud project, billing, and ongoing maintenance. Third-party widgets (e.g. Elfsight) are another paid option. Our approach: highlight the rating here and link to Google Maps for the live list.
