The repair

Winding mechanism non-functional. Found three cracked internal gears preventing operation; brittle plastic spring hooks also needed repair. Process: full disassembly, identify failed gears, source/replace gears, lubricate mechanism, repair spring hooks, reassemble. Outcome: wind-up works; feet and tail move again. Work period: started 3 May 2026 over several days. Client approved sharing repair story and video links.

About this item

Vintage Illco (ILLCO) Walt Disney Donald Duck wind-up preschool toy (client-owned). Illco Toy Co. (Hong Kong / China manufacture common on preschool Disney line; company acquired by Tyco in 1992). Typical era: 1970s-1980s plastic wind-ups; some examples dated ~1987+ when Disney branding shifted to The Walt Disney Company. Related Illco Donald variants seen on the collector market include Donald The Musician (wind-up musical) and a Donald Duck rocking-horse wind-up musical; Mickey counterparts include walking wind-ups (feet/legs move, some play music) and Peek-a-Boo crib toys (Hong Kong-made, moving feet/ears). This unit: wind-up drives animated feet and tail. Materials: plastic shell, internal metal wind-up spring, plastic gears and spring hooks. Market: common vintage preschool Disney collectible; used examples often trade in modest range on eBay/auction sites (exact model/year - confirm from underside Illco / Made in Hong Kong (or China) stamp if present). Sources: Disleelandia Illco history; eBay/PicClick Illco preschool listings.
