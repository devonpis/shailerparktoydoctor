The repair

This large floppy brown-and-cream dog arrived tired and flat, with grubby pile and seams that needed attention rather than a full rebuild. We gave the plush a thorough clean, added fresh stuffing to restore its shape, opened the fabric along the existing seam line, and stitched the skin closed again so it lies neatly and is ready to cuddle.

About this item

Brown and cream floppy-eared dog plush in a lying pose: shaggy medium-brown pile on body, head, and long ears; smoother tan/cream velour muzzle, forehead patch, and paw tips; dark brown fabric nose and small black bead eyes. Likely generic retail or gift plush from the 1990s–2000s in lines similar to Russ or Aurora. Non-electronic—polyester pile and fiberfill only; sewn tags help confirm maker when present.
