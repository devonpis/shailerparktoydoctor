The repair

This Red Octane Guitar Hero World Tour wireless guitar would not power on reliably — the fault traced to poor contact at the battery terminals.

Reaching the battery compartment meant a full disassembly of the guitar shell, which is fiddly on this model. We cleaned and rebuilt the contacts, reassembled the neck and body, and confirmed stable power for play.

About this item

Red Octane wireless Guitar Hero World Tour guitar controller for Microsoft Xbox 360 (model 95457.805; sunburst red-to-black metallic finish, white pickguard). Five coloured fret buttons (green/red/yellow/blue/orange), touch-sensitive neck slider strip (World Tour / GH5 era), strum bar, whammy bar, Star Power / Back button, Xbox guide-compatible D-pad cluster. 2.4 GHz wireless (receiver dongle not shown in hero). Manufacturer: Red Octane (Activision publishing era). Market: legacy rhythm-game peripheral; used sunburst units common on eBay. Note: game console, not a toy—electronics and switch repair typical.
