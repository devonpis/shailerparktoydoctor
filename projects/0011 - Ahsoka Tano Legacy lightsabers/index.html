Disney Parks Ahsoka Legacy lightsaber set — battery contact repair on both curved hilts after careful disassembly and reassembly so sound and blade lighting work again.

The repair

Both hilts in this Ahsoka Tano Legacy lightsaber set had lost reliable power — classic symptoms of corroded or sprung battery contacts in the AAA compartment.

Each hilt was carefully disassembled to access the battery box and contact strips. We cleaned and reseated the terminals, then rebuilt the hilts and checked sound and blade lighting before hand-back.

About this item

Disney Parks Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge Ahsoka Tano Legacy Lightsaber Set (shopDisney / Walt Disney Imagineering collectible, released 2023). Pair: single-blade saber (~14.5 in hilt) and shoto saber (~10.3 in hilt), both curved silver hilts with grey side panels and white diamond accents; white translucent blades (Ahsoka-specific; not interchangeable with standard Legacy blades). Features: motion and clash sensors, film-style hum/clash SFX, rechargeable/removable blades, 3× AAA per hilt. Character design from Star Wars Rebels / The Mandalorian / Ahsoka (white blades). Ages 14+. Market: premium sold-out collectible; hilt-only and signed LE variants also exist. Repair scope likely electronics, blade contact, or battery box.
