Jakks 48-inch First Order Stormtrooper Battle Buddy — battery contact repair after a long disassembly of the giant figure so motion detection and voice lines work reliably again.

The repair

This Jakks Pacific First Order Stormtrooper Battle Buddy had gone quiet — no motion-triggered lines even with fresh batteries, pointing to the internal battery contacts. The battery pack sits deep inside a 48-inch shell, so the job meant a slow, careful disassembly of the whole figure. We serviced the contacts and wiring at the box, then rebuilt the trooper and tested walk-by responses before return.

About this item

Jakks Pacific Star Wars: The Force Awakens First Order Stormtrooper Battle Buddy (Colossal Big-Figs; about 48 in / 122 cm tall). Full-body white and black First Order armour sculpt with poseable blaster-arm elbow. TRY ME retail packaging indicates motion presence detection with movie lines and sound effects. UPC 039897908335 is documented. Moulded plastic shell with an internal battery compartment—typical service work is wiring, the presence sensor, or corroded contacts.
