Anpanman Kotoba Zukan DX talking book and pen — battery contacts, switch, and connector work after patient disassembly and testing to clear an intermittent sound fault.

The repair

This Sega Toys Anpanman Kotoba Zukan DX set came in with an intermittent fault — sometimes the talking pen and book responded to the pages, sometimes not, which made the problem harder to pin down.

We disassembled the pen and book case, cleaned and rebuilt the battery contacts, replaced a tired switch and connector, and worked through trial-and-error testing until touch prompts and vocabulary sounds played consistently again.

About this item

Sega Toys (Sega Fave) アンパンマン おしゃべりいっぱい! ことばずかんDX / Anpanman Oshaberi Ippai! Kotoba Zukan DX (Word Encyclopedia DX; released 2017). White briefcase-style electronic book with orange carry handle; cover art Anpanman and friends; bundled yellow Anpanman-head touch pen (おしゃべりペン) with power button—touch illustrations to hear Japanese words and English (cover notes 英語もしゃべるよ). ~2,200+ vocabulary, ~50 character voices, quiz and song modes; picture book pages inside. Power: 2× AA (typical). Successor lines include Super DX and 2021 Premium (more words, recording). Market: popular Japanese preschool educational toy; DX out of production but common second-hand. Repair: pen contact, speaker, or battery corrosion. Source: Sega Toys product listings / kotoba zukan comparisons.
