The repair

This Sega Toys Anpanman Kotoba Zukan DX set came in with an intermittent fault — sometimes the talking pen and book responded to the pages, sometimes not, which made the problem harder to pin down. We disassembled the pen and book case, cleaned and rebuilt the battery contacts, replaced a tired switch and connector, and worked through trial-and-error testing until touch prompts and vocabulary sounds played consistently again.

About this item

Sega Toys (Sega Fave) Anpanman Kotoba Zukan DX (Word Encyclopedia DX; 2017): white briefcase-style electronic book with orange handle, cover art of Anpanman and friends, and a yellow Anpanman-head touch pen. Touch illustrations to hear Japanese words and English phrases; about 2,200 vocabulary entries, character voices, quiz and song modes, and picture pages inside. Power is typically 2× AA; later Super DX and Premium revisions added more content and recording features.
