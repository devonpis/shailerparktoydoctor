The repair

The bunny needed a full refresh: the plush had picked up soil and the nose area had worn or split enough to need rebuilding rather than a quick surface clean. We deep cleaned the fabric and pile, repaired and shaped the nose, then restuffed the body for an even fill and a natural floppy bashful stance.

About this item

This grey bashful bunny is a soft plush rabbit in the contemporary nursery/gift style that has been widely sold since the 2000s. The "bashful" naming convention is common across boutique plush ranges and usually indicates a floppy, long-limbed rabbit designed for cuddling and display. Construction is generally polyester plush fabric with polyester fiber fill and safety-stitched facial details. Market value is usually in the everyday gift-plush range, with stronger prices only when a recognized brand tag, original swing tag, and clean condition are present.
