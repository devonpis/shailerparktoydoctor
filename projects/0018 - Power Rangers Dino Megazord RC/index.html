Work in progress

The repair

The Megazord's radio handset and internal controller path were failing — intermittent response and dead sectors pointed to worn switches and a damaged control cable rather than only the drivetrain. We replaced the controller circuit board, installed a fresh controller switch where the original had failed, and replaced the control cable end-to-end. After reassembly and testing, the RC link was stable across the full stick travel.

About this item

Power Rangers Dino Megazord RC toys come from the long-running licensed Power Rangers product ecosystem tied to Bandai-era and later reissue cycles. Dino-themed Megazord variants were strongest in the 1990s and 2000s collector memory, with radio-control spin-offs appearing in multiple regional toy lines. Most units use injection-molded ABS/PVC body parts, simple geared drivetrains, and a battery-powered RC control board. Market value depends heavily on completeness (controller, battery cover, decals), with boxed and fully functional examples attracting the strongest buyer interest.
