The repair

The bear arrived with failed or missing eyes and several fabric failures — tears and a hole through the plush shell that needed proper closing, not a single surface patch. We replaced the eyes with a secure child-safe approach for the piece, then worked the tears and hole closed with matched stitching and reinforcement so the onesie pattern lines up and the fill stays contained.

About this item

This bear in a giraffe onesie is a novelty costume plush style common in gift shops and seasonal retail from the 2000s onward. The design combines a standard teddy body with character-costume clothing cues, which is typical of modern mixed-animal plush concepts. Materials are usually polyester plush, stitched applique facial details, and lightweight fiber fill with embroidered accents on the onesie. Secondary-market pricing is generally modest unless the item can be matched to a specific licensed range or a known plush maker via sewn labels.
