Work in progress

The repair

The figurine had surface grime and the music box movement had dried or sticky pivots, so the melody dragged or hesitated instead of keeping an even tempo. We deep cleaned the exterior without damaging the paint, then opened and serviced the internal music box module — correct lubrication on the comb and drive train — so the mechanism runs freely for seasonal display.

About this item

Christmas bear music box figurines are part of holiday giftware traditions that became especially common from the late 20th century onward. These pieces were sold through card shops, seasonal home-decor retailers, and collectible Christmas catalog lines. They are often polyresin or ceramic with a small internal wind-up musical movement and painted holiday detailing. Value is usually decorative rather than rare-collector level, but complete and working music box mechanisms help retain interest for seasonal display buyers.
