The repair

Both KFC Chicky footballer figures arrived with neck breaks — each had snapped into two pieces at the neck. We bonded the breaks with epoxy, filled the cracks with filler, sanded smooth, and finished with matt white paint to match the original colour on both figures.

About this item

KFC Chicky footballer figurines are promotional fast-food character items, typically produced as campaign tie-ins for seasonal sport events. This style of giveaway/meal premium was especially prominent from the 1990s through 2010s in quick-service marketing. They are usually molded PVC or ABS mini figures with painted team-themed details and simple fixed poses. Collector interest is niche but real for complete campaign sets, while single loose figures generally trade in the affordable promo-memorabilia range.
