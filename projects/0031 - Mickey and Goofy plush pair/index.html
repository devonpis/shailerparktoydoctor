This licensed Mickey and Goofy plush pair needed fabric work on Goofy’s trousers and reliable power at Mickey’s battery contacts so both companions are display-ready again.

Work in progress

The repair

Goofy’s trousers had torn seams and worn fabric that needed rebuilding so the outfit sits neatly on the body again. We repaired the trouser fabric and stitching to match the original shape. Mickey’s sound or electronics would not trigger reliably because the battery contacts had oxidized and lost connection. We cleaned and renewed the contact path so power reaches the module consistently.

About this item

This Mickey and Goofy plush pair is licensed Disney character merchandise from the long-running theme-park and retail plush ecosystem. Pairings of core Disney characters are common across 1990s to current-era releases from Disney Store and partner manufacturers. Most examples use polyester plush, embroidered facial features, and soft internal fill with occasional bean weighting.
