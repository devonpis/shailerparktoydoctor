This Miniso radish plush had grubby pile and split seams — we deep cleaned the fabric, closed the tears, and restuffed the body so the character looks plump and cuddly again.

Work in progress

The repair

The radish had picked up everyday grime and several tears in the outer fabric where stuffing was starting to show. We deep cleaned the plush without damaging the embroidered face. Torn seams were repaired and the body was restuffed with fresh fill so the shape is even and firm again for display or cuddling.

About this item

This Miniso radish plush is a modern lifestyle-store novelty plush from the MINISO retail era that expanded globally in the 2010s and 2020s. Food-vegetable character plush became a recognizable category in Asian-inspired gift merchandising during that period. Construction is usually soft polyester fabric with polyester fill and embroidered face details aimed at decor-plus-cuddle use.
