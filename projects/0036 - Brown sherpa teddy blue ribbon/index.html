Work in progress

The repair

The bear had dull, grubby sherpa and several holes and tears where stuffing was escaping. We deep cleaned the fabric and patched the damaged areas so seams stay closed under use. Missing or damaged eyes were replaced with safe matching parts, and the body was restuffed so the teddy sits upright and feels even again. The blue ribbon was checked and left neat at the neck.

About this item

This brown sherpa teddy with blue ribbon is a gift-plush bear in the textured sherpa-fleece style that became common in the 2000s onward. Ribbon-neck teddy formats are a staple of florist, pharmacy, and department-store plush assortments. Materials are usually polyester sherpa fabric, polyester stuffing, and satin or grosgrain ribbon trim.
