A Jellycat Blossom bunny needed ear and paw fabric repairs plus a thorough clean — we restuffed the body so the floral Bashful line character looks soft and even again.

Work in progress

The repair

The bunny’s ear fabric and paw panels had torn or worn thin at the seams. We repaired those textile areas and matched the floral ear panels as closely as the original construction allowed. The plush received a deep clean, then fresh stuffing so the limbs and head have an even, cuddly shape without lumpy gaps.

About this item

Jellycat Blossom Bunny is a recognized modern plush line from Jellycat London, a UK brand founded in 1999 and known for soft contemporary nursery designs. Blossom variants are notable for their floral-print ear and foot fabric accents within the Bashful family style. Construction uses high-quality polyester plush with embroidered facial details and soft fill; floral panels are usually cotton blend prints.
