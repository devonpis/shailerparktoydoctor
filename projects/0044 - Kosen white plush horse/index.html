This Kosen white plush horse had face and body damage — we patched the holes, remade an ear and eyebrow, fitted new eyes, and closed tears so the realistic German plush looks complete again.

Work in progress

The repair

The horse had holes in the body and face where stuffing was showing, plus tears along stressed seams. We patched those areas and closed the tears with matching pile direction. A damaged ear and eyebrow were remade, replacement eyes were fitted, and the head was shaped so the expression reads naturally again for display.

About this item

Kosen white plush horse is associated with Kosen (Koesen), the German plush maker established in 1912 and known for naturalistic animal design. Kosen pieces are often produced to a higher realism standard than mass-market plush lines. Construction may include premium synthetic or natural-look pile fabrics, carefully shaped forms, and firm but soft stuffing for display-quality silhouette.
