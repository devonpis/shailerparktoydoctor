About this item

Ty Beanie Boo husky plush belongs to Ty's Beanie Boo line launched in 2009, recognized by oversized glitter-style eyes and named character releases. The line became a major modern collectible category with regular seasonal and retailer-exclusive drops. Materials are typically polyester plush with polyester fill and Ty's branded heart/swing tags identifying character name and birthday.
