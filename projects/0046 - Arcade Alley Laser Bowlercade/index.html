The repair

The Arcade Alley Laser Bowlercade would not reload bowling pins—the mechanism jammed and the motor could not overcome the load. The design puts high torque on the plastic stud and rod. We modified those parts to reduce friction and torque demand so the pin reset cycles smoothly again.

About this item

Arcade Alley Laser Bowlercade is a home tabletop bowling lane sold under the Arcade Alley brand (often JAKKS Pacific in recent retail). The set is a long plastic alley with nine pins, balls, and an automatic ball return — aimed at solo play or two-player scoring at home. Typical features include electronic scoring, automatic pin reset, and arcade-style sound effects.
