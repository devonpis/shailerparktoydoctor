The repair

This Toy Story Woody pull-string doll would not pull or trigger the sound box when the cord was drawn. We repaired the spring mechanism inside the pull-string housing so the voice box triggers reliably again.

About this item

Toy Story Woody pull-string dolls are licensed Pixar/Disney products, with major production by Thinkway Toys across multiple film generations. The pull-string voice format became iconic from the original 1995 Toy Story release onward. Construction usually combines a soft body with molded plastic head/hands/boots, stitched costume textiles, and an internal voice pull-cord module.
