About this item

Yellow Yoshi plush is licensed Nintendo character merchandise from the long-running Super Mario universe, with color variants representing different Yoshis across game eras. Plush releases have appeared continuously since the late 1990s through current lines. Typical materials are polyester plush and fill with embroidered eyes/details and franchise tagging from authorized distributors.
