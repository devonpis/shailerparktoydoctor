The repair

This John Deere ride-on tractor had electronic faults that needed board-level work. We partially disassembled the toy for access, repaired the circuit board, and reassembled so drive and features work again.

About this item

John Deere ride-on tractor toys are licensed child ride-ons produced across multiple manufacturers (including pedal and battery-powered variants) from the late 20th century onward. They mirror real John Deere styling and are a long-standing farm-toy category. Common construction uses heavy-duty molded plastic body panels, steel axles, rubber/plastic wheels, and in powered models a 6V/12V battery drive system.
