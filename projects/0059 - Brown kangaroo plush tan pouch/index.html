About this item

This brown kangaroo plush with tan pouch is in the Australian souvenir-animal plush category, widely sold through tourism and gift channels for decades. Kangaroo plush designs are usually generic unless tags identify a specific wildlife brand or museum line. Typical materials are polyester faux-fur fabrics, polyester fill, and stitched details around muzzle, paws, and pouch.
