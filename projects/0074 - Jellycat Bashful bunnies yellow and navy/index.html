The repair

Two Jellycat Bashful bunnies—a yellow and a navy—needed fabric work and stuffing refresh. We deep cleaned and restuffed both. The yellow bunny needed an arm reattached; the blue bunny needed a leg reattached so both sit and display correctly.

About this item

Jellycat Bashful bunnies in yellow and navy are from Jellycat London's flagship Bashful rabbit range, a highly recognizable modern plush line since the late 1990s. Colorway variation is a major part of Jellycat collecting, especially when shades retire. These bunnies are typically polyester plush with soft fill and minimal embroidered features for a clean nursery look.
