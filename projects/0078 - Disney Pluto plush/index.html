About this item

Disney Pluto plush is officially licensed Mickey universe merchandise, produced across many decades by Disney Store and approved partner manufacturers. Pluto's design has remained consistent, making tag/date cues important for pinpointing release era. Most versions use polyester plush with embroidered or appliqued facial details and soft fill suited to child handling.
