The repair

This elephant baby lovey blanket needed structural fabric work as well as cleaning. We deep cleaned the piece, remade trim, replaced worn fabric, machine-sewed new sections, and repaired holes and tears.

About this item

Elephant baby lovey blankets are comfort-security items that combine a small plush head with a soft blanket panel, a format widely adopted in nursery markets from the 2000s onward. They are usually sold as infant gifting essentials rather than collector toys. Materials commonly include polyester minky/velour or cotton-blend cloth, with embroidered facial details and lightweight fill in the head section.
