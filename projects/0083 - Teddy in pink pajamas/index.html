About this item

A teddy in pink pajamas is a bedtime-themed gift plush style that became especially common in department and card retail channels from the late 1990s onward. Pajama bears are often seasonal or occasion-linked (new baby, get well, bedtime gift). Construction usually combines polyester plush body fabric with stitched textile pajama garments and soft internal fill.
