About this item

Paddington Bear plush is licensed from Michael Bond's character first published in 1958, with long-running toy production by multiple official licensees such as Eden, Rainbow Designs, and others by region/era. Signature identifiers include duffle coat, hat, and often the marmalade tag motif. Materials are typically polyester plush or mixed textile construction with dressed clothing components and embroidered facial features.
