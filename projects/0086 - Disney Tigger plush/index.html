The repair

This Disney Tigger plush had both fabric damage and electronic faults. We deep cleaned the plush, repaired internal circuit bad contacts, reinforced wiring and solder joints, and repaired cuts and tears in the fabric.

About this item

Disney Tigger plush is licensed Winnie the Pooh character merchandise from Disney's long-established plush programs. Tigger has been issued in many fabric and expression variants, so tags and production details are important for release-era matching. Most versions use striped polyester plush, embroidered face detailing, and soft fill with a shaped tail.
