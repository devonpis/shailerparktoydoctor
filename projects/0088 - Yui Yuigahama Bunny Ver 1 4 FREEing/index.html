The repair

The front hair section on this Yui Yuigahama Bunny Ver. 1/4 FREEing figure had broken away and the piece was lost, leaving an obvious gap in the sculpted hairline that would show on display.

We reconstructed the missing section from plastic sprue, reshaped it to match the original profile, and attached it to the figure with UV resin. The repair was sculpted and sanded smooth, then repainted with layered glazing so the colour and sheen blend with the factory hair finish.

About this item

Yui Yuigahama Bunny Ver. 1/4 by FREEing is a large-scale Japanese anime collectible figure from FREEing's B-style bunny line, based on the character from My Teen Romantic Comedy SNAFU (Oregairu). FREEing's 1/4 scale releases are known for oversized shelf presence and premium display orientation.

The figure is typically PVC/ABS construction with painted detail work, and many FREEing bunny releases include real fabric fishnet stockings as a signature mixed-material element. Market demand is collector-driven and can rise after production ends, with box condition, authenticity, and complete accessories strongly affecting resale.
