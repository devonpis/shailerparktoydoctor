About this item

This brown sherpa teddy with pink ribbon is a contemporary gift-plush bear in a common florist/card-shop style. Sherpa-texture bears in pastel ribbon variants have been mass-retail staples from the 2000s onward. Construction is usually polyester sherpa with polyester fill and decorative ribbon trim around the neck.
