The repair

This Buzz Lightyear figure had corroded batteries, a failed board, and loose arm joints. We repaired battery contacts, transplanted a replacement circuit board and arm from a donor unit, touched up paint, cleaned the shell, and secured the loosening arm joint.

About this item

Buzz Lightyear figures are licensed Toy Story collectibles released continuously since 1995 across basic, talking, and deluxe formats. Major versions were produced by makers including Thinkway and Mattel-license channels depending on era and region. Most figures use molded ABS/PVC construction with painted decals, articulated joints, and in electronic variants integrated light/sound boards.
