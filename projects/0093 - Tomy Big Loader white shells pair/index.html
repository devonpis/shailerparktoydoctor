Pair of Tomy Big Loader white shell bodies — cracked clip tabs and seam repairs so both units mount securely on the shared blue power chassis used in our Big Loader track set (see project 0014).

The repair

Two white Tomy Big Loader shell bodies from the same system as the blue motorized chassis (project 0014). Both had stress cracks at the mounting clips and loose upper/lower shell seams that prevented a firm fit on the power unit.

We plastic-welded and reinforced the clip points, aligned the arrow markers for travel direction, and test-fitted each shell on the restored blue chassis so they couple and run without rattling.

About this item

Pair of Tomy Big Loader white plastic shell bodies (same Japanese Tomy Big Loader / Big Big Loader system as the blue motorized chassis in project 0014). Each shell: white upper/lower clamshell, arrow on top indicating travel direction, rear coupling peg, front hitch — mounts on the shared power-unit footprint at track stations. This job covers two white units of the same model (not the single blue/teal power chassis). Era and market: 1977–1990s Tomy construction set; shells often need clip tabs, motor contact through chassis, or cosmetic crack repair. Related repair: 0014 — one blue power unit.
